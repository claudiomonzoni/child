WHO WE ARE

ALGUN TEXTO AQUI

“World Organization of Child Ambassadors of Peace”, also known as “The Cultural Embassy”, is a 501(c)(3) non-confessional, multicultural nonprofit organization with a special focus on Cultural Diplomacy through the Performing Arts with Headquarters in Washington, DC, USA, whose mission is to raise awareness and spread a Culture of Peace and Understanding between the Peoples of the World.

To achieve this goal, we work with children of the most diverse social, cultural, ethnic, religious, and economic backgrounds, coming from across the United States as well as from all the continents and regions around the globe.

Our organization, works in partnership with our branches overseas, all the international diplomatic missions, foreign governments through their Ministries of Culture and Ministries of Foreign Affairs, as well as with prestigious cultural and performing arts institutions around the world.

Highlighting children's intellectual, spiritual, emotional, and artistic virtues is crucial for an enhanced, healthier and stronger self-esteem, which shall guarantee a secure and successful adult, no matter the field of action.

We strongly believe that children are the leaders of the future, and therefore, as such, they shall be the mentors of the upcoming generations.

All the children and teenagers who decide to join us are bestowed upon the honorary title of Child Ambassadors of Peace.

THE CULTURAL EMBASSY

OPENING DOORS IN 2021

The Cultural Embassy will be open for the entire community on a daily basis from Monday through Friday. Here, children and teenagers are professionally trained in a large and varied array of cultural and artistic disciplines ranging from foreign languages, ceremonial and etiquette, children's and human rights, to musical theory, singing, dancing, and acting.

All those who decide to join us at The Cultural Embassy will be sharing precious time with others in an unmatched and loving atmosphere of multiculturalism while reinforcing our organization's ethos of everyone coexisting peacefully finding the richness of our uniqueness in diversity.

We will engage themin top-notch international musical productions through which we spread the values of acceptance, understanding, friendship, brotherhood, compassion, empathy, tolerance, altruism, forgiveness, and freedom, as we believe they represent the purest manifestations of one and most important thing: universal love.

Our wish is to accompany all children with extraordinary abilities in the field of the performing arts by training them and boosting their talents up to the maximum level of proficiency, while introducing them to the arena of diplomacy.

THE MUSICAL SUMMIT

This fraternal union of multiethnic young artist reaches its pinnacle in the making of The Musical Summit,a never-before-seen international event. This annual show, in the form of a series of gala concerts, features 10 original songs representing the US and foreign nations performed by their respective soloists in their role as child ambassadors especially selected for the occasion, and backed by a highly talented ensemble made up of the children and teenagers members of The Cultural Embassy.

These prodigies are accompanied on stage by a fantastic 16-piece orchestra, and Ezekiel Elkin, a renowned young pianist, Warner Music and ASCAP award-winning songwriter, philanthropist, and advocate for children's rights.

NEW EDITION 2020 IN CONTEXT OF COVID-19 | THE MUSICAL SUMMIT RESHAPED

The Cultural Embassy in partnership with Warner Chappell Music, the world’s largest publishing company, in the current context of a global crisis triggered by the COVID-19 pandemic, in which staying home is paramount to safeguard our health as well as our neighbor’s, has the pleasure to announce the opening of a call for children and teenagers, from all around the globe to participate in a one-of-a-kind quest to find the solo performers of the new original song to be included in the upcoming all-English album by the renowned musical group, “Zeke & The Kiddos”, entitled “The Musical Summit”.

Yes! Ezekiel Elkin, the founder and president of The Cultural Embassy is the "Zeke" in the band!

To accomplish this, all interested songwriters must send in their original and unpublished songs –previously protected and registered at ASCAP or their respective authors and composers’ societies– by means of an online form by attaching a demo with a simplified arrangement and the lyrics. The songs must be written in one of the official languages of the country, nation, or people that the songwriter wants to represent and performed preferibly, but not necessarily, by a child.

Once the external songs are chosen by The Cultural Embassy’s Selection Committee, they shall be publicly announced on our website and our official social network accounts, indicating title, names of original songwriters, and country of representation. After this, the official “Zeke & The Kiddos” musical arranger, Gerardo Gardelín, an amazingly gifted Broadway musical director for Latin America, shall dress the said songs with his magical work. Elkin, also a masterful polyglot, will undertake the versioning of the foreign songs into English.

THE QUEST FOR THE CHILDREN

The children and teenagers who wish to participate in this international online audition need to create a personal profile, at no cost, by filling out an enrolment form upon having read and agreed to the rules and regulations accompanied and assisted by their parents or tutors.

This initiative born in the heart of Ezekiel Elkin, calls together all children and teenagers between 5 and 15 years old from all around the globe, regardless of gender, ethnic background, nationality, religion, and social, economic or cultural status.

1

First stage: Public notice and reception of all the material from the opening of the call and online platform until the 11.59 p.m. of the date of deadline. Participants shall send a video performing a song of their choice in their own mother tongue, which allows them to deploy their talent to the fullest extent.

Simultaneously, they shall post and share the said video in their social networks including the hashtag #ImaKiddo and #TheMusicalSummit2020.

2

Second stage: A Selection Committee made up of a remarkable group of experts in the field of the performing arts and music industry professionals at an international level shall proceed to assess each and every video received and do a first pre-selection, in a democratic, pluralist, public, and transparent way, within the period established by the organization. All selection work shall be broadcast and publicized on and through all existing channels.

3

Third stage: Those in this phase, shall choose a song exclusively from the first “Zeke & The Kiddos” album released by Warner Music on Spotify and other platforms and streaming services. Yes, the album is in Castilian. But fear not, we shall provide the children with the lyrics. After all, singing in a foreign language is part of the challenge. At the same time, they shall be routed to the YouTube official channel to enjoy and share with their families the exclusive videos from the “Zeke & The Kiddos” World Tour.

4

Fourth stage: Having narrowed down the group of children and teenagers in the run, the production shall, at its sole discretion, and based on the profiles, vocal ranges, and other attributes of the participants, allot the unpublished songs to them, so as to assess who of them, respectively, fits best for each musical piece of which they shall be the recording soloist.

It is worth highlighting that the leading contestants upon being definitely chosen shall proceed to record, covered by the production, their respective songs at a professional Warner Chappell Music recording studio in the performer’s country or city of residence, under the direction, live and in a remote way, of Ezekiel Elkin, through Zoom or Skype. Right after, their songs shall be mixed and mastered in the United States of America for the subsequent album’s worldwide release. Depending on how the COVID-19 crisis unfurls and how economy evolves, a series of groundbreaking concerts could potencially follow.

The young soloists shall be backed in all songs, by the world-famous and outstandingly awarded One Voice Children’s Choir of Salt Lake City, Utah, under the direction of prodigious director, Masa Fukuda.

During all three selection stages, those who dare not participate as singers, and by extension their respective families, parents, siblings, cousins, grandparents, friends, are welcome to join us by creating their own account to become a virtual member –yet not less real– of the Selection Committee, whose public participation shall be in representation of the audience's diversity pertaining to the most varied and rich cultures of the world under the premise “one person, one vote”, in equal conditions with the rest of the specialized members. Even every single like given to contestants in platforms like YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram, shall be counted.
